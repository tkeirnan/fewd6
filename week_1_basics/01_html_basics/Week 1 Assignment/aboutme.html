Tommy Keirnan in a Nutshell

  • Patrick Keirnan, Tommy's soon-to-be father, an Irish-American, native to Illinois, decides to travel to South America on a motorcyle.
  • Father meets Tommy's mother, Silvia, in the highest capital city in the world.
  • Patrick and Silvia fall in love and get married.
  • Tommy is born in the highest capital city in the world.
  • Patrick gets hired by the United States Agency for International Development and hence..
  • Tommy attends highly recognized American International Schools.
  • Tommy finishes college, works, and saves to travel the world.
